About Me

My first love was guitar, and I currently hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in music with special concentration in Classical Guitar from Kennesaw State University. While pursuing my musical career, I found myself always interesting the new technologies coming out and I started to become more involved with computers because of this. Thank you so much Chris and Anne for getting me here, and continuing to help me grow even further.
